We use an evidence-based curriculum which encourages children to learn through their experiences with individual attention from their teachers to guide their development.We provide a safe, loving and inspiring space for your child to learn, grow and make new friends.

Programs

Infant-Tolder Program

Ages:6 months-3 years

Focus

-Building trusting relationships
-Implementing a caregiving rountine

Infants and toddlers start learning from birth continue at an exponential rate. Key to their learning is developing trusting relationships with adults who follow certain routines to ensure that children feel safe and loved.

Our day care program includes a daily routin with devlopmentally appropriate stimulation which allows your little ones to discover the world them using their bodies and senses.

Pre-School Program

Ages:3-6 years

Focus

- Learning Through Personal experiences

- Following Daily Routines.

Pre-schoolers learn best by having fun. Our pre-school in Table View offers a well-developed programme aimed at encouraging children to learn through various forms of play.

They are placed in a loving, safe and stimulating environment with professional guidance in order to support individual growth.

From using their bodies to crawl, run and jump to understanding the world through their senses, they are guided through their experiences to help develop their view of the world and their unique personalities and interests

After School Programs

Ages 6-9 years

Focus

- Outdoor plays and physical activities.
- Offering guidance with homework and assignments

After a busy day at school children need a safe and stimulating space which they can use to explore and play freely or just take a break and spend time reading or studying in a peaceful environment. At our aftercare center, we provide children with a nutritious meal, a much-needed space to rest, guidance with homework and the freedom to explore our indoor and outdoor play areas safely.
