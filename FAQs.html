Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the average cost of your daycare for one child?

You pay once off fifty rand registration fee at the beginning of each year, then five Hundred Rand each month that includes work books and transport

How many teachers are in a daycare?

We currently have Ten employees in our day care we have about Six qualified teachers and four general workers that look after Eighty children

What time does the daycare open?

Our daycare operates from eight oClock in the morning until Four oclock in the evening we also offer after care from Five oclock until late at an extra low cost we charge it per hour

What is the right age for child to start at daycare centre?

From 3months to 5years of age
